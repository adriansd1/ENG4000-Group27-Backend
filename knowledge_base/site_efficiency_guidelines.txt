Energy efficiency across telecom sites is driven by monitoring, automation, and predictive maintenance.

Key efficiency drivers include:
- Intelligent HVAC control systems.
- AI-based anomaly detection for power spikes.
- Real-time monitoring of energy consumption.
- Automated shutdown of unused equipment.

Sites should be ranked by energy intensity and operational criticality.

Priority should be given to:
1. High-traffic urban sites.
2. Core switching infrastructure.
3. Regions with high electricity costs.

Toronto and Vancouver are considered Tier-1 critical infrastructure zones due
to the high economic and operational impact of outages.
