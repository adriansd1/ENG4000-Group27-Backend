Our company operates telecom infrastructure across all Canadian provinces, including
Ontario, British Columbia, Alberta, Quebec, and Atlantic Canada.

The national energy strategy focuses on:
- Reducing carbon emissions across all telecom sites.
- Increasing the adoption of renewable energy sources.
- Improving operational efficiency and reducing power costs.
- Standardizing energy monitoring across all regions.

Regional differences in climate and energy markets affect power consumption:
- Cold regions require higher heating loads.
- Urban regions require more cooling and backup systems.
- Remote sites rely heavily on diesel generators and battery systems.

The company aims to achieve net-zero emissions by 2040 through a combination of
energy optimization, renewable energy, and infrastructure modernization.
