Backup power is critical to telecom resilience. The company uses a mix of:
- Lithium battery storage.
- Diesel generators.
- Hybrid renewable backup systems.

Toronto and other major cities require enhanced redundancy due to higher outage risk
and economic impact.

Future improvements include:
- Transitioning to hydrogen fuel cell backup systems.
- Integrating battery storage with smart grid infrastructure.
- Predictive maintenance of backup assets.

Urban infrastructure should always maintain at least N+1 redundancy.
